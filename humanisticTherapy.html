What is Humanistic Therapy ?


Humanistic Therapy is a psychological treatment approach that emphasizes the individual's inherent potential for self-growth
and self-actualization. It focuses on understanding the person's subjective experience the importance of personal responsibility and
free will. Key principles include empathy, unconditional positive regard, and aiming to create a supportive and non-judgmental
enviroment. humanistic Therapy is particularly effective for enhancing self-esteem, improving interpersonal relationships,
and fostering personal growth and self-awarness.
