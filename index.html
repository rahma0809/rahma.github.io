Why is therapy important for Mental Health ?


Therapy is really important for mental health. It helps because you can talk about your feelings and problems in a safe place.

The therapist listens and does not judge you.They can help you understand why you feel the way you do and how to cope with difficult emotions better.

Therapy can also teach you new skills to handle stress and improve how you communicate with others. It can also make your relationship with others better.

Overall, therapy gives you tools to feel better about yourself and make positive changes.


When to consult a therapist ?


You should consider consulting a therapist if you're feeling sad, anxious, or angry often.
Therapy can also help if you're having trouble dealing with stress from work or relationship, going through big changes in life, or having ongoing problems in your relationship.

If you notice changes in your behavior like being more irritable, withdrawing from others, or doing things that aren't good for you, therapy can be helpful.

It's also a good idea to consider therapy if you've been through a tough experience that's still bothering you, or if you've any physical problems like headaches or trouble sleeping that might be related to how you feel inside.
