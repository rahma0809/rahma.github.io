What is Motivational Therapy ?


Motivational Therapy is a counseling approach aimed at helping individuals find the motivation to chnage harmful behaviours.
Techniques include open-ended questions, reflective listening, affirmations, and summarizing. It is particularly effective for substance abuse,
health behavior changes, and mental health issues.By fostering a non-judgmental and supportive enviroment. It helps clients recognize their
own reasons for change and build confidence in their ability to achieve it.
