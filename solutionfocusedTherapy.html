What is Solution-focused Therapy ?


Solution-focused Therapy is a breif, goal- oriented therapeutic approach that emphasizes finding solution rather than exploring problmes.
The therapist and client work collaboratively to set clear, attainble goals and develope practical strategies to reach them.
Techniques include asking scaling questions, exploring past successes, and invioning a preferred future. SFT is practiculary effective
for individulas seeking quick and practical resolutions to specfic issues, fostering empowerment and positive change within a short timeframe.
