What is Trauma Therapy ?


Trauma Therapy is a specialized form of psychotherapy designed to help individual process and heal from traumatic experiences.
It focuses on addressing the emotional, psychological and physiological effects of trauma. The goal is to help individuals integrate
traumatic memories, reduce symptoms such as anxiety and depression, and restore a sense of safety and control.
Trauma therapy provides a supportive and empathetic environment, enabling clients to explore and resolve trauma-related issues,
ultimately promoting recovery and improvrd mental health.
